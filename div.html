Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of computers, networks, software, and other electronic devices to process, store, secure, and exchange all forms of digital data. It is the backbone of modern society, driving innovation and efficiency across nearly every sector, from business and education to healthcare and entertainment. In a commercial context, IT systems facilitate essential functions like data management, internal and external communication, and workflow automation, leading to enhanced productivity and operational effectiveness On a personal level, IT is deeply woven into daily life, enabling instant global communication through smartphones and the internet, online banking, e-commerce, and personalized entertainment experiences.
